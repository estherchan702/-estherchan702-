Page Design and User Experience:

Maintain a clean, aesthetically pleasing, and user-friendly page design with coordinated color schemes and reasonable layout. Ensure clear navigation so users can easily find content of interest.

Optimize page loading speed by avoiding excessive use of images and videos that may slow down loading times.

Add appropriate interactive effects such as mouse hover effects and scrolling animations to enhance user experience, but use them moderately to avoid impacting page performance and readability.

SEO Optimization:

Implement SEO optimization by selecting appropriate keywords and optimizing page titles, descriptions, and content to improve personal webpage rankings in search engines, increasing the chances of being discovered by potential employers or partners.

Ensure webpage URL structures are clear, concise, and include keywords to help improve search engine understanding and indexing.
